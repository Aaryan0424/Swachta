About this site
We welcome everyone to our site , which is solely made for the purpose for spreading awareness about the perks of Swatchta. Swatchta or cleanlinesss is the need of the hour and not maintaining it has led to many misfortunes for our country. This was always a a concerning issue for us, which was later highlighted by our honourable Prime Minister at a global level. Our PM Mr Narendra Modi started the movement of Swatch Bharat Abhiyan on the date of Gandhi Jayanti(2nd Oct 2014) which had the sole motive to make                                                                                                our country clean and spread awareness among the citizens.
Below are some factors which deeply affect our Swactch Bharat Mission.
Monitoring usage of toilets
Behaviour Change
Toilet technology
Operation and maintenance of school toilets
Technological solutions for safe disposal of menstrual waste
Early decomposition of faecal matter
